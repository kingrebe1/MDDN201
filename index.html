Medium Citylink Manhole Cover

Citylink are a company which began in the 1990s and installed fibre-optic cables for internet in the CBD in 1996. The cables originally ran along the trolley bus overhead system before it was dismantled.

Street Markings

These spray painted street markings can often be seen around Wellington. There appears to be a colour-coded system for gas, water, electricity, internet and telecommunications. Internet and telecommunication related markings are usually purple.

Chorus Marking

Presumably the Chor in this marking refers to Chorus, the company that builds and maintains the majority of New Zealand's internet network.
